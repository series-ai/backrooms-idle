## Notes for Generation

- **Color palette:** Yellows (#E8DCA0, #C4A44A), blacks, dark browns, muted greens, occasional harsh red/orange for danger
- **Texture:** Grain, VHS scan lines, slight blur, analog noise
- **Mood:** Liminal, unsettling, lonely, found-footage
- **Size:** Icons should work at small sizes (64x64 to 128x128) — keep details bold and readable
- **Background:** Transparent PNG preferred for all icons so they layer cleanly over the dark UI
